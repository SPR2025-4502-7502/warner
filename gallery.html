My busy weekend!

My Saturday on April 18th was a busy day! It was Record Store Day, which is a celebration of independent record stores. My band, Pink Houses, got invited to play on the roof of local record store Hitt Records. The day was also Vinylthon, a 24 hour non-stop broadcast of exclusively vinyl records. The student ran radio station I'm in, KCOU, participated in this nationwide fundraiser for college stations. After my band's set, I DJed for two hours outside of the store, then went and hung out with other DJs the rest of the event.

Thanks for looking at my page.
