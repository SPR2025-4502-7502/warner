7 Albums that Fuse Country with Indie Rock

Indie rock is a big blanket term for alternative music. It encompasses music that is released independently (meaning outside of a record label/deal), typically includes guitars, bass and drums (at the least) and takes influences from multiple genres. This list will look at indie rock albums that fuse in a lot of country influence specifically.
